Neighbours

Tim Winton • Short Story Unit 1

"Neighbours" by Tim Winton explores the transition of a young couple moving into a multicultural suburb and learning to accept differences.

Summary

The story begins with a newlywed couple moving into a neighborhood populated by European immigrants. Initially, they are prejudiced and wary of their loud, expressive neighbours. However, as they struggle with the challenges of daily life and eventually pregnancy, the neighbours offer overwhelming support and kindness.

Theme: The story highlights the breaking down of cultural barriers and the realization that shared human experiences (like birth) transcend cultural differences.

Questions & Answers

  • Q: How does the couple's perception change?
    A: They move from viewing the neighbours as "aliens" to seeing them as a supportive community.
