Unit 1: Critical Thinking

Last Updated: Dec 2025 • Language Development

Critical thinking improves your ability to analyze, evaluate, and interpret information to make reasoned judgments.

Reading Comprehension

In this unit, we explore various texts that challenge conventional wisdom and encourage looking at problems from multiple perspectives. Questions typically ask you to identify the author's bias, the underlying assumptions, and the strength of the evidence provided.

Exercise Tip: When answering critical thinking questions, always support your argument with specific examples from the text.

Grammar & Writing

Focus area: Analyzing arguments and writing persuasive essays.
