Unit 2: Network & Data Communication

Last Updated: Dec 2025 • Unit 2

Fundamentals of computer networks, transmission media, and network topologies.

2.1 Introduction

A computer network is a group of computers linked to each other that enables the computer to communicate with another computer and share their resources, data, and applications.

LAN

Local Area Network: Covers a small area like a room or a building.

WAN

Wide Area Network: Covers a broad area, often across cities or countries (e.g., Internet).
