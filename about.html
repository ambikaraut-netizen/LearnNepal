Our Mission

Empowering Students with Premium Education

LearnNepal is dedicated to providing high-quality, structured, and accessible digital notes for NEB Class 11 & 12 students across Nepal.

The LearnNepal Story

Education in the digital age should be beautiful, fast, and accessible. We noticed that most educational resources were cluttered, outdated, or hard to navigate.

We started LearnNepal with a simple goal: to create the "Apple" of educational websites for Nepali students. A place where learning feels premium, notes are meticulously crafted, and the user experience is seamless.

Quality Content: Verified by subject experts.
Zero Ads Clutter: Focus on reading, not closing popups.
Always Free: Education helps everyone.
